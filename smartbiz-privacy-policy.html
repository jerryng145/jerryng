Privacy Policy for SmartBiz AI Sales Brain

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartBiz AI Sales Brain ("SmartBiz," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when people interact with our business through Facebook Messenger and related business communication channels.

1. What SmartBiz AI Sales Brain Does

SmartBiz AI Sales Brain helps our business receive customer enquiries, respond to messages, assist with product or service questions, and improve follow-up communication through an AI-assisted sales workflow.

2. Information We May Collect

When you message our business through Facebook Messenger or related Meta services, we may receive:

  • Your public profile information provided by Meta, such as your name or profile identifier.
  • The content of messages you send to our business.
  • Timestamps, message metadata, and conversation context necessary to respond.
  • Technical and usage information provided by Meta or service providers for security, diagnostics, and service operation.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information we receive to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Generate AI-assisted replies for sales, product, setup, and follow-up questions.
  • Review conversations to improve response quality, business operations, and customer experience.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our business policies.

4. AI Processing

Some messages may be processed by AI systems and service providers that help us generate draft or automated replies. We use this processing only to operate and improve our customer communication workflow.

5. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as reasonably necessary with:

  • Technology and hosting providers that help us operate SmartBiz AI Sales Brain.
  • Messaging, automation, analytics, or AI service providers used to run our business workflow.
  • Legal authorities or other parties when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for customer support, business records, service improvement, security, and legal compliance, after which it may be deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or other control over your personal information. You may also stop messaging our business at any time.

9. Third-Party Services

Our services may rely on third-party platforms such as Meta and other infrastructure providers. Your use of those platforms is also subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

SmartBiz AI Sales Brain is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this service.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
